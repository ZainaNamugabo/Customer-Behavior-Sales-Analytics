# Customer-Behavior-Sales-Analytics

I built a customer behavior and sales analytics dashboard designed to turn raw data into clear, actionable insights for smarter decision-making. Instead of just presenting numbers, this dashboard tells a story—helping businesses understand who their customers are, how they behave, and why they stay or leave

At a glance, stakeholders can track core performance metrics such as total revenue, churn rate, customer lifetime (tenure), and overall customer distribution. The dashboard goes further by breaking down these metrics into meaningful segments, making patterns easy to spot and act on.

# What this dashboard delivers

Revenue breakdowns across contract types, tenure groups, and payment methods

Churn analysis highlighting risk areas by customer segment and behavior

Customer segmentation based on demographics and subscription patterns

Trend visibility to uncover retention opportunities and growth drivers

Clean, executive-ready design that communicates insights without complexity

# Goal

This project is built with a focus on clarity, usability, and business impact—so both technical teams and executives can quickly understand performance and make informed decisions.

# Ultimately, the dashboard helps answer critical questions like:

Where are we losing customers—and why?

Which segments generate the most revenue?

What factors influence long-term retention?
